Remote Work Policy

Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Remote work requests must be approved by your manager at least 2 days in advance.

Vacation Policy

Full-time employees receive 20 paid vacation days per year.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 1 week in advance.

Expense Reimbursement

Employees can submit expense reports for business purchases.
Reimbursement requests must include a receipt and be submitted within 30 days.
